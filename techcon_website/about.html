History

TechCon began in 2012 as a small developer meetup in San Francisco. Over the years, it has grown into a global gathering of technologists, engineers, and thinkers, uniting to discuss and shape the future of technology.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire innovation, encourage collaboration, and spotlight cutting-edge developments in the tech world. We aim to create a space where developers, designers, entrepreneurs, and tech enthusiasts can connect and grow.

Past Speakers

Dr. Maria Patel: AI researcher and ethics advocate. She spoke about fairness in machine learning and the importance of inclusive AI.

Tom Lee: Creator of DevForge. His session on scaling developer platforms drew the largest crowd in 2023.

Angela Chen: Cybersecurity expert and TED speaker. She covered emerging digital threats and data protection trends.
